About Read•Able

Read•Able is a news platform designed to make daily information easy to read and understand for people of all age groups. In a world filled with complex language and overwhelming news, Read•Able focuses on clarity, simplicity, and accessibility.

This project is built with accessibility in mind. We use clear layouts, readable font sizes, appropriate spacing, and straightforward language to allow people with reading difficulties, dyslexia, impaired vision, and a strong desire to read have a comfortable time consuming news.

Read•able is an open-source project and is continuously evolving. Future improvements include reading modes, dark mode, and category-based content to further enhance the reading experience.
